Punah-पुस्तक

"Everyone deserves a second chance, so does Books".

Essence

Punah-पुस्तक’s essence is to create an online marketplace for buying and selling second-hand books, offering affordable prices and doorstep delivery, while fostering environmental sustainability through book reuse.

  • Learn More

Driving force

The driving force behind Punah-पुस्तक was the desire to make quality books accessible and affordable, while promoting environmental sustainability. The project offers a convenient platform for buying and selling second-hand books, encouraging reuse and eco-friendly practices.

  • Learn More

From Concept to Code: Our Hackathon Journey

Punah-पुस्तक was developed during the Hackaccino hackathon organized by Devfolio, where my team of four dedicated members worked collaboratively to bring this idea to life. The hackathon provided a dynamic environment that fostered creativity and problem-solving, allowing us to focus on crafting a user-friendly platform for second-hand book transactions.

We combined our skills in web development, SEO, and team management to design and implement the platform. Our approach involved building a seamless interface for buying and selling books, integrating convenient doorstep delivery, and ensuring environmental sustainability through the reuse of books. The project not only met the hackathon’s challenge but also aimed to positively impact both book enthusiasts and the environment.

Discover What Sets Punah-पुस्तक Apart

Punah-पुस्तक features a comprehensive platform for second-hand books, enhancing the buying and selling experience with diverse selections, affordable prices, easy listing, convenient doorstep delivery, and a focus on eco-friendly practices through book reuse and recycling.

  • Diverse Collection: Extensive range of genres and subjects.
  • Affordable Prices: Budget-friendly book options.
  • Easy Selling: Simple listing and buyer connection.
  • Convenient Delivery: Hassle-free doorstep service.
  • Eco-Friendly: Promotes book reuse and reduces waste.
